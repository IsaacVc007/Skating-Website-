Simon Palowski

Simon Palowski, originally from Łódź, Poland, developed a passion for skateboarding at a young age. He spent countless hours perfecting tricks and participating in street competitions, quickly becoming known in his local skateboarding community. At the age of 16, Simon won the Łódź Street Jam, a prestigious local competition that attracted the best skaters from across the city. His standout performance, with a perfect combination of technical tricks and creativity, helped him earn the title and solidified his reputation as one of the top underground talents in Poland.

After moving to Buenos Aires, Argentina, at the age of 23, Simon continued to pursue his love for skateboarding and decided to give it a go in smaller local competitions. He competed in several grassroots events, gaining valuable experience and further honing his skills while connecting with the passionate skating scene in the city. His time in Buenos Aires deepened his connection to the culture, and his admiration for the Boca Juniors football team, from the neighborhood of La Boca, inspired his vision for Casa Skate.

Simon’s journey from Łódź to Buenos Aires not only shaped his skating career but also fueled his desire to share the joy of skateboarding with others, leading him to create Casa Skate, where quality boards and a love for the sport come together.
